Save on leasing costs with pre-tax payments

  • Get an instant quote

What is a Novated Lease?

Essentially, it's a three-way agreement between you, your employer and a finance company that bundles together all the costs of your vehicle and allows you to pay them using pre-tax dollars.

Explainer of what the video is about

2:30

What are the perks of novated leasing?

For you, the main one is financial. Because your lease payments can be deducted from your income, you'll be able to save big bucks on your tax return at the end of the year. You'll also have full control over which vehicle you choose and if you decide to leave your job further down the track, you'll be able to take it with you.

  • Save on your tax
  • Choose the vehicle that's right for you
  • Enjoy a higher disposable income
